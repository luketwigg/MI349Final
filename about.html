About Us

Our Mission

At EduVacation, we believe that traveling with friends should be affordable and stress-free so students can focus on their education. We help students find the best travel deals tailored to their needs, ensuring that every vacation is memorable with maybe a few familiar faces. Your next adventure is just a click away…

What We Do

EduVacation connects students with great travel deals for popular vacation destinations at their university. Whether it’s booking flights, finding accommodations, or planning itineraries, we take care of the details so you can focus on making memories. Our platform offers exclusive discounts for student groups, ensuring you get the best deals without the hassle.

Why Choose Us?

We understand that planning a trip with classmates can be complicated and time-consuming. That's why we handle the logistics, making it easier than ever to coordinate your next getaway. Most importantly, with EduVacation you can enjoy the perfect trip without breaking the bank!
